    Bridge

    Before I share some of my designs for bridge as well as the winning structures, here is some advice.

  • First, allow yourself a lot of time to test multiple designs
  • Second, make sure you read the parameters VERY carefully so you don't get disqualified (like three other teams in our division)
  • Third, test different kinds of wood, such as balsa and bass
  • Fourth, make sure you do a basic stress test on your final bridge so it doesn't collapse right away (whoops)
